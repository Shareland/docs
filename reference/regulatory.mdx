# Regulatory & Legal

## Risk disclosure

Trading SQFT tokens involves significant risk. Real estate prices can go down as well as up. You may lose some or all of your funds. Do not trade more than you can afford to lose.

## Nature of SQFT tokens

SQFT tokens are synthetic derivative instruments designed to track the price per square foot of residential real estate. They do not represent ownership of real property, shares in a property, or any security backed by real estate.

## Jurisdiction restrictions

Shareland is not available to residents of the United States or other restricted jurisdictions. By using the exchange, you confirm that you are not located in, incorporated in, or a citizen of any restricted jurisdiction.
