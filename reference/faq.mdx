# Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Shareland available in my country?">
    Shareland is currently available to users outside the United States and other restricted jurisdictions. During sign-up, you'll be asked to confirm your jurisdiction. Users in blacklisted countries will not be able to access the exchange.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the minimum investment?">
    There is no minimum investment on Shareland. You can buy any dollar amount of SQFT tokens in any market.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to trade?">
    Shareland charges a flat 1.5% fee on each transaction (buy or sell). There are no other fees — no management fees, no withdrawal fees, no hidden charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I own actual real estate?">
    No. SQFT tokens are synthetic instruments that track the price per square foot of real estate. You gain price exposure without owning physical property. Think of it like commodity futures — you can gain exposure to oil prices without owning barrels of oil.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is SQFT priced?">
    SQFT tokens are priced by Shareland's oracle, which aggregates real estate transaction data and listing prices in real time. The methodology was developed with Wharton School researchers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose money?">
    Yes. SQFT tokens reflect real estate prices, which can go down as well as up. Only invest what you can afford to lose. Shareland is a speculative instrument — please read all risk disclosures before trading.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a market halts?">
    If the oracle detects an anomaly, a market may temporarily halt. You'll receive an email notification. Your tokens remain in your wallet and are not affected by a halt. Markets resume once the situation is resolved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I withdraw my funds?">
    Sell your SQFT tokens and the proceeds will be returned to your connected wallet. From there you can transfer to any external wallet or exchange.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Shareland regulated?">
    SQFT tokens are structured as derivatives/synthetic instruments. Shareland is working toward compliance with applicable regulatory frameworks. Please see our Terms of Service and risk disclosures for the full legal picture.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
