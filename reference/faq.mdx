# Frequently Asked Questions

## Platform

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Shareland available in my country?">
    Shareland is not available to residents of the United States or other restricted jurisdictions. During sign-up, you'll be asked to confirm your jurisdiction. Users in restricted countries will not be able to access the exchange.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the minimum investment?">
    There is a **$0.10 minimum per transaction**. No maximum — invest any amount above that.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to trade?">
    Shareland charges a flat **1.5% fee** on each transaction (buy or sell). No management fees, no withdrawal fees, no hidden charges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I own actual real estate?">
    No. SQFT tokens are synthetic instruments that track the price per square foot of real estate. You gain price exposure without owning physical property — similar to how commodity futures let you gain exposure to oil prices without owning barrels of oil.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is SQFT priced?">
    SQFT tokens are priced by Shareland's oracle, which aggregates real estate transaction data and listing prices in real time. The methodology was developed with Wharton School researchers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I lose money?">
    Yes. SQFT tokens reflect real estate prices, which can go down as well as up. Only invest what you can afford to lose. Please read all risk disclosures before trading.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Token Mechanics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if a market halts?">
    If the oracle detects an anomaly, the market may automatically halt. You'll receive an email notification. Your tokens remain in your wallet and are unaffected. Markets resume once the anomaly is resolved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if USDC depegs?">
    If USDC depegs by more than 1% from USD, the Shareland protocol will automatically halt trading. You can still modify your collateral position to prevent accidental liquidation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I withdraw my funds?">
    Sell your SQFT tokens and proceeds return to your connected wallet. From there, transfer to any external wallet or exchange.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is my wallet secured?">
    Shareland uses Privy for secure, non-custodial key management. You control your keys — Shareland never has custody of your funds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Shareland regulated?">
    SQFT tokens are structured as synthetic derivative instruments. Shareland is working toward compliance with applicable regulatory frameworks. See our [Terms of Service](https://share.land/terms) for the full legal picture.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
