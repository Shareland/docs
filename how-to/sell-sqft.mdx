# Sell SQFT Tokens

## Step 1: Open your portfolio

Navigate to your portfolio to see all open positions.

## Step 2: Select a position

Click on the position you want to exit. You'll see your current value, entry price, and unrealized gain/loss.

## Step 3: Enter your sell order

Click **Sell** and enter the amount to sell - either a specific number of tokens or a dollar amount. You can sell your full position or a partial amount.

## Step 4: Confirm

Review the transaction details including the 1.5% fee and confirm. Funds are returned to your wallet immediately after the transaction confirms on-chain.

<Note>
  Partial sells are supported - you don't have to exit a position all at once.
</Note>
