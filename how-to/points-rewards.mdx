# Points & Rewards

Shareland rewards active participants through a points system. Points can be earned by trading, holding positions, and engaging with the community.

## How to earn points

- **Trading** - earn points on every buy and sell transaction
- **Holding positions** - earn points for maintaining open positions over time
- **Community** - earn points for posting in neighborhood community tabs
- **Referrals** - earn points when you refer new users to the platform

## Viewing your points

Your points balance and leaderboard rank are visible in the exchange. Click the **Rewards and leaderboard** button in the top navigation to see your current standing.

## Seasons

Shareland runs points programs in seasons. At the end of each season, points may be converted to rewards. Season details including start/end dates and reward structures are announced by the Shareland team.

<Note>
  Season 1 is wrapping up. Season 2 launches at mainnet on March 10, 2026. Stay tuned for details on Season 2 rewards.
</Note>

## Renting rewards

In addition to trading points, Shareland also distributes **Renting Rewards** - a share of protocol revenue distributed to token holders. Your renting rewards balance is visible in your portfolio under **Renting Rewards** and can be claimed at any time.
