# Understanding the Oracle

The Shareland oracle is the engine that prices SQFT tokens. It aggregates real estate transaction data to produce a live, reliable price per square foot for each market.

## Data sources

The oracle pulls from multiple data sources including recorded home sales and listing data across US markets. Data is weighted by recency, transaction volume, and geographic relevance.

## Price updates

Oracle prices update on a regular cadence. You'll see price charts reflecting these updates in real time on the exchange.

## Market halts

If the oracle detects an anomaly — such as a sudden price spike that falls outside expected parameters — the market may automatically halt to protect investors. Halts will be displayed in the exchange UI and we may send notifically depending on your account notification settings.

Markets resume once the anomaly is investigated and resolved.

## Methodology

Shareland's oracle methodology was developed in collaboration with researchers at the Wharton School. The approach uses academically validated models for neighborhood-level real estate pricing.
