# Buy SQFT Tokens

## Before you start

Make sure you have:
- A completed Shareland profile
- Funds in your connected wallet

## Step 1 — Browse markets

From the exchange, browse available markets. Each market card shows:
- Current price ($/sqft)
- 24h price change
- Market name and location

## Step 2 — Select a market

Click on any market to open the detail view. Here you'll see:
- Live price chart
- Historical price data
- Neighborhood information

## Step 3 — Enter your trade

Click **Buy** and enter the amount you'd like to invest. The interface will show:
- Number of SQFT tokens you'll receive
- 1.5% transaction fee
- Total cost

## Step 4 — Confirm

Review the details and confirm your trade. The transaction processes on-chain — this typically takes a few seconds.

<Check>
  Your new position will appear immediately in your portfolio.
</Check>

## Daily trade limits

A daily trade limit applies per wallet during the launch phase. If you hit the limit, you'll be notified in the interface.
