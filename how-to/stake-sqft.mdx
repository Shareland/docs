# Stake SQFT Tokens

Staking lets you earn yield on your SQFT token positions. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed at any time.

## Step 1: Open a market

From the exchange, click any market card to open the detail panel.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Markets">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/markets.png" alt="Shareland markets view" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Open the Stake tab

In the market detail panel, click **Trade** then select the **Stake** tab.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Stake panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/stake.png" alt="Stake panel" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Enter your amount

Enter the number of SQFT tokens you want to stake. The panel shows your current staking position and estimated rewards.

## Step 4: Confirm

Review and confirm the transaction. Your staked tokens remain in your portfolio and continue to track the oracle price — staking does not lock your exposure.

<Note>
  Staking rewards are shown in the **Portfolio** tab under **Staking Rewards** and can be claimed at any time.
</Note>
