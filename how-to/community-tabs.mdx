# Community Tabs

Each neighborhood on Shareland has a community tab where investors can share insights, analysis, and information about that market.

## Navigating to the community tab

1. Go to the **Explore Markets** page on [share.land](https://share.land)
2. Select a neighborhood you're interested in
3. In the top sliding bar, select **Community**

You'll see posts from other investors, sortable by **Newest**, **Trending**, or **Most Popular**.

## Community guidelines

To keep Shareland's community welcoming and useful for everyone:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be respectful">
    Treat everyone with courtesy. Hate speech, harassment, or discrimination will not be tolerated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep it relevant and appropriate">
    Post only content relevant to the neighborhood or market. Avoid obscene or graphically disturbing material.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No spam or self-promotion">
    Engage in genuine conversations. Avoid spamming, advertising, or excessive self-promotion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Protect sensitive information">
    Never share private keys, proprietary information, or other sensitive data in community posts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Report vulnerabilities privately">
    If you discover a security vulnerability, report it privately to the team — do not post it publicly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reporting issues

If you witness a guideline violation or encounter something that makes you feel unsafe, contact the team via [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/5njaDH5y39).
