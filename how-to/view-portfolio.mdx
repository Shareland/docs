# View Your Portfolio

Your portfolio is your hub for tracking everything — open positions, total value, rewards, and transaction history.

<img src="/images/screenshots/portfolio.png" alt="Shareland portfolio dashboard" />

## Portfolio tabs

| Tab | What you'll find |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Assets** | All your open SQFT positions and their current value |
| **Watchlist** | Markets you're tracking but haven't invested in yet |
| **Activity** | Full history of your buy, sell, and reward transactions |

## Reading your positions

Each position in the Assets tab shows:
- **Market** — which SQFT token you hold (e.g. SQFT\_NYC)
- **Tokens held** — how many SQFT tokens you own
- **Current value** — your position's value in USDC at the live oracle price
- **Gain/loss** — unrealized P&L since your entry price

## Portfolio Value

Your total **Portfolio Value** (in USDC) is the sum of all your open positions at current oracle prices. This updates in real time as market prices move.

## Renting Rewards

The **Renting Rewards** section shows protocol revenue distributed to your wallet. Click **Claim** to withdraw your rewards to your wallet balance at any time.

## Buying Power

Your **Buying Power** shows how much USDC you have available to invest. Add funds by depositing USDC to your connected wallet.
