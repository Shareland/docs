# View Your Portfolio

Your portfolio dashboard shows all your active positions and performance.

## What you'll see

- **Total portfolio value** — current value of all open positions
- **Total gain/loss** — unrealized P&L across all positions
- **Individual positions** — each market you hold tokens in, with current price and change

## Reading a position

Each position card shows:
- Market name and location
- Tokens held
- Entry price vs current price
- Unrealized gain/loss ($ and %)

Scroll down to see a full log of your buy and sell activity, including timestamps and fees paid.
