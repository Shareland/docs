## Step 1: Go to share.land

Visit [share.land](https://share.land) and click **Log In** in the top right corner.

## Step 2: Sign up

A Privy popup will appear. You can sign up with your email (a 6-digit verification code will be sent), or connect an existing Web3 wallet such as MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet. Privy automatically creates a secure wallet for you if you sign up with email — no seed phrases or browser extensions needed.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Connect wallet">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/markets.png" alt="Shareland markets with Log In button" />
</Frame>

Complete your profile (name + accepting the Terms of Service) to unlock trading.

<Note>
  You'll need USDC on the Base network to start trading.
</Note>

## Step 3: Start exploring

Once connected, you're ready to browse markets and make your first trade.
