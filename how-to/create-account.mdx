# Create an Account

## Step 1 — Go to share.land

Visit [share.land](https://share.land) and click **Get Started**.

## Step 2 — Connect your wallet

Shareland uses [Privy](https://privy.io) for secure wallet management. You can:
- Connect an existing wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
- Create a new wallet directly — no separate app needed

## Step 3 — Complete your profile

To unlock trading, complete your profile:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Accept the Terms of Service and risk disclosures

<Warning>
  Profile completion is required before you can buy or sell SQFT tokens. This is a compliance requirement.
</Warning>

## Step 4 — Start exploring

Once your profile is complete, you can browse markets and make your first trade.
