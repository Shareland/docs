# Borrow Against Your Position

Shareland lets you borrow USDC against your SQFT token holdings — unlocking liquidity without closing your position.

## Step 1: Open a market

From the exchange, click any market card to open the detail panel.

## Step 2: Open the Borrow tab

In the market detail panel, click **Trade** then select the **Borrow** tab.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Borrow panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/borrow.png" alt="Borrow panel" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Enter your amount

Enter the USDC amount you want to borrow. The panel shows:
- Your available collateral (SQFT token value)
- Loan-to-value ratio
- Estimated interest rate

## Step 4: Confirm

Review the terms and confirm. Borrowed USDC is sent to your connected wallet immediately.

<Warning>
  If your collateral value drops below the liquidation threshold, your position may be partially liquidated to repay the loan. Monitor your position regularly.
</Warning>
