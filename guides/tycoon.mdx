# Tycoon AI Agent

Tycoon is Shareland's built-in AI agent — your personal real estate investment analyst available directly on the exchange.

## What Tycoon does

Tycoon gives you real-time intelligence to make smarter investment decisions:

- **Market insights** — analysis of neighborhood trends, price movements, and oracle data
- **Portfolio analysis** — understand your current positions and exposure
- **Data synthesis** — combines oracle data, transaction history, and demographic information
- **Trade assistance** — helps you understand the implications of a trade before you make it

## How to access Tycoon

Tycoon is available directly within the Shareland exchange. Look for the Tycoon AI interface within any market or portfolio view.

## The $TYCOON token

$TYCOON is the utility token associated with the Tycoon AI agent within the Virtuals Protocol ecosystem. Transaction fees on the Shareland exchange flow into a buyback and burn program for $TYCOON holders.
