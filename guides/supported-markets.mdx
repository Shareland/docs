# Supported Markets

Shareland currently supports the following markets at launch. Each market tracks the price per square foot of residential real estate in that geography.

## Available Now

Markets are added on a rolling basis. Check the exchange at [share.land](https://share.land) for live prices.

<Note>
  Market availability depends on oracle data coverage. We prioritize markets with high data density and liquidity.
</Note>

## How markets are added

New markets go through a process:

1. **Data review** — sufficient transaction data must exist for reliable oracle pricing
2. **Oracle validation** — prices are backtested and validated by Shareland's data team
3. **Launch** — market goes live with initial liquidity

## Request a market

Want to see a specific neighborhood or city? Reach out to the team or post in the community Discord.
